OUR TEAM

How we met

We all are gathered thank to our educational institution Salesianos of Huesca. Tatiana is economy and english teacher. Anastasia and Maria are two students from 4ESO. We have already worked together in the project of Technovation and sure we were incredibly content with the work of each other. When we find out about the competition of Wisibilizalas, we knew that we can't lose this opportunity. The objective of the Wisibilizalas was very similar to our worldview, so we were deligthted by the opportunity to find out more about the women from the STEM areas who are near us.

Tatiana Labarta

My name is Tatiana Labarta and I am a teacher of secondary education at Salesianos Huesca. My main interest areas are English and Economy. I studied International Economics at Zaragoza and Maastricht University and afterwards I got a degree in Translation and Interpreting. My passion is education and also technology. I try to link them in my classes. Technology has the power to engage students in learning. I have taught at Zaragoza University in several fields such as Econometrics, Maths and also English. I have done research about international organisations and during 10 years I have worked as an admin-manager and researcher of an NGO related to Regional Economic Research. My interest in technology grows each day and I think it is of huge importance to use it in schools. In our school, each student has their own laptop and we work with the ICTs constantly. The students of this team, Maria and Anastasia, have instigated a genuine revolution in our school, the STEM revolution. New opportunities to develop technological projects in our center have arisen with them.

Maria Delgado

My name is María de los Ángeles Delgado and I'm 15 years old. I was born in Colombia but I've lived here, in Spain for 11 years. I'm in 4th of ESO due to I've skipped one grade. I love studying, my hobbie can de defined as "learning new things" My favourite subject is Mathematics and I love number theory and calculus. I also like physics; specially quantum physics, astrohpysics and nuclear physics. I've been a student at Qubit by Qubit, a course of quantum computing offered by The Coding School, IBM and MIT. Furthermore, I love learning about neuroscience. I'm also a graphic designer! My notes and posters about the fields I study are considered perfect by the students who use them. I'm also writing some books, one of them is science fiction and the other ones, hopefully, will be useful for all the students around the world. I'm the CEO and founder of MeteoriteGlobal, a community for students, teachers, people who want to learn and people who want to teach. I'm the Student Ambassador of Spain of Technovation, and I've participated two years in this program, creating and coding two apps.

I learn everything by myself, I learnt Web-Development last year, during quarantine but since I was 7 years old, my father introduced me into the world of technology, we started with Arduino! This opportunity is the perfect project to show my skills on HTML5, CSS3, JavaScrpit and Design! It's good to see that there are opportunities for students, related to science and Technology. Let's get as high as a Meteorite!
